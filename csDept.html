Department of Computer Science

About the Department

The Computer Science Department at George Mason University (GMU) is renowned for its comprehensive range of undergraduate and graduate programs, including Bachelor's, Master's, and PhD degrees in Computer Science and related fields. With a strong emphasis on inclusivity and diversity, the department aims to foster a collaborative learning environment that encourages innovation and creativity. The faculty consists of distinguished researchers and educators who are committed to excellence in teaching and mentorship. The department offers cutting-edge curriculum focusing on critical areas such as software engineering, information systems, cybersecurity, and artificial intelligence. In addition to academic programs, the CS Department at GMU actively promotes industry collaboration through partnerships with local tech companies and organizations, providing students with valuable real-world experience and networking opportunities. With a commitment to "Computing for All," the department strives to increase representation in computing and welcomes students from diverse backgrounds to explore the dynamic field of computer science.

Programs

  • BS, MS, and PhD in Computer Science
  • MS in Software Engineering
  • MS in Information Systems
  • MS in Information Security and Assurance
  • BS in Applied Computer Science (Various Concentrations)
